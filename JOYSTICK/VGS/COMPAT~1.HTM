Titles on this list were judged as playable by our Quality Assurance department. While they have been recommended, the titles may exhibit some defects. For instance, in order to keep up with the flow of the game, CVGS may need to occasionally skip a frame in the video presentation.

NOTE: All games on the CVGS for Windows recommended games list were tested using the recommended system requirement of a 400 MHz or faster processor and a video card that supports 24-bit color (True Color) or higher.
